MidEval Submission Requirements 
The MidEval submission consists of three primary components: a revised abstract, a 
red-teaming peer-review exercise, and an outline of the proposed paper. 
1. Revised Abstract & Red-Teaming 
Following the previous evaluation’s methodology, you will engage in a  red-teaming activity  : 
●  Exchange:  Share your revised abstract with a partner  team. 
●  Critique:  Receive critical feedback and structured  comments from your peers. 
●  Refinement:  Incorporate this feedback to produce a  polished version of your abstract. 
2. Skeletal Paper Outline 
You are required to submit a comprehensive skeletal outline of your proposed research paper. 
This will serve as the blueprint for your final submission. 
●  Requirement:  Each section must contain  rough pointers  and bulleted descriptions 
detailing the specifics of that particular section. 
●  Structure:  Your outline should mirror the traditional  components of an ACL long paper. 
3. Formatting Guidelines 
●  Template:  Use the official  ACL Long Paper LaTeX  template  .
